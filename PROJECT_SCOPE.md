# Project Scope (AGIPragma)

## What this is
AGIPragma is a research program studying developmental intelligence under:
- World Shifts (Birth Events),
- Disconnected autonomy (no central supervision),
- Bounded plasticity and stability constraints.

## What this is not
- Not a claim of “solved AGI”.
- Not a weaponization effort.
- Not a promise of near-term productization.
